A Study of Studies¶

In AnyBody, all types of analysis, whether pure mechanical system analysis, musculoskeletal analysis or design analysis, are carried out by objects that are referred to as “studies”.

Studies have operations that can be executed to perform the analysis. The objective of this tutorial is to explain the sequence of a typical study from its mechanical system information, to the initial conditions, kinematics to the inverse dynamic analysis.
